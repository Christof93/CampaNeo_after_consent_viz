\begin{abstract}
Data privacy scandals in the recent past have left many people in the western world insecure about the implications of data sharing in every day life. 
At the same time advances in machine learning and data science have given us the possibility to enhance safety and quality of life for many people.
To collect data from individuals nowadays companies have to rely on mutual consent and trust. New regulations make sure that consumers are not exploited or taken advantage of.
From a law standpoint it is necessary to inform customers to a full extent about what information is collected from them.
In this paper we present a new approach to keep people informed about the activities linked to their data sharing agreements. We introduce a sample application with a transparent visualisation and helpful functionality to give users a better understanding of the data sharing processes in the background of their consent agreements.

\end{abstract}
